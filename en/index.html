Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our GPS tracking service

How much does GPS installation cost? +
GPS installation costs $130,000 COP (approximately $35 USD) as a one-time payment. This includes the GPS device on loan, professional installation, complete configuration, and training on how to use the Monitor-X app.
What's the monthly service fee? +
The monthly service is $45,000 COP (approximately $12 USD). This includes 24/7 real-time tracking, 30-second updates, 12-month history, unlimited geofences and alerts, Monitor-X app access for iOS and Android, and technical support.
What is geofencing? +
Geofencing lets you create virtual boundaries on the map (like your home, office, or warehouse). Every time your vehicle enters or exits these zones, it's recorded with the exact date and time. You receive instant alerts and can view the complete entry/exit history.
Can I shut down my vehicle's engine remotely? +
Yes! Through the Monitor-X app, you can remotely shut down your vehicle's engine with a single tap. This immobilization feature is essential in case of theft or emergency. You can also view the complete history of when the vehicle was started and stopped.
Does the GPS work throughout Colombia? +
Yes, our GPS system works throughout Colombian territory wherever there is cellular network coverage. We cover Bogota, Medellin, Cali, Barranquilla, Cartagena, and all cities from La Guajira to the Amazon.
Do you track motorcycles? +
Yes, we offer specialized GPS tracking for motorcycles with all the same features: real-time location, speed monitoring, geofencing, and remote engine shutdown. Perfect for delivery services, couriers, and personal motorcycle security.
What certifications do you have? +
Seguimiento Activo is certified by MinTIC (Ministry of Information Technologies and Communications) and is an authorized provider for the Colombian National Police for satellite tracking of vehicles and heavy machinery.
Can I track a commercial fleet? +
Absolutely! Our system is perfect for fleet management. Track all your vehicles from a single dashboard, monitor driver behavior, optimize routes, and generate detailed reports. We offer special pricing for fleets of 5 or more vehicles.
