### ONE MANUAL STEP REQUIRED:

**Generate og-image.png from og-image.svg**

The SVG file exists and looks professional, but social media platforms (Facebook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp) require PNG format.

**Quick Method** (5 minutes):
1. Go to https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png
2. Upload `og-image.svg`
3. Set output to 1200x630 pixels
4. Download as `og-image.png`
5. Place in root directory

**Alternative**: See `GENERATE-OG-IMAGE.md` for 4 other methods.

**Why This Matters**: Without the PNG, social media shares won't show your professional preview image, reducing click-through rates by 50%+.
